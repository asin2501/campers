LASER PRECISION

MEETS A PASSION FOR ADVENTURE

The completely engineered monocoque design ensures this purpose-designed camper covers all the bases.
Creating this work of art starts with a state of the art manufacturing facility coupled with an all Australian build quality and access to the hardest testing grounds in the country. Backed by a 5 year structural warranty, the X1 will make it home, every time.

LIGHTWEIGHT

aluminum BODY

FORGE-TECH

Hot dip galvanised chassis
Independant Trailing Arm Suspension
The dual trailing arm independant suspension delivers a plush travel and features a spring design. Compared to a usual leaf sprung setup, this delivers a smooth, precise ride by keeping the tires in constant contact with the ground
Optional AirBag Suspension
Provides a convenience like no other, with dual controls you can raise/lower each side of the X1 to level regardless of where you are parked. Digging holes or chocking wheels to get your bed or benchtops level are a thing of the past.
EFS Dual Shock Absorbers
EFS Big Bore 35mm dual shock absorbers as standard to prevent frequent heat-induced shock fade caused by long trips over corrugated or rough roads.
Integrated Water Tanks
Integrated single or dual rotor-moulded food grade polyethylene water tanks supply the X1’s water through food grade hoses.
Industry Leading Ground Clearance And Wheelbase
The Patriot Campers X1 is built upon a 1875mm wheel track and 475mm of ground clearance. The chassis and suspension combo smoothes the harshest terrain and delivers unmatched control and comfort without sacrificing agility.
Laser Cut Interlocked Construction
The X1’s chassis is a true work of art, featuring a combination of steel RHS sections and sheetmetal components entirely laser cut and interlocked prior to welding ensures the ultimate strength.
Hot Dip Galvanised
Protects steel from corrosion by providing a thick, tough, metallurgically bonded zinc envelope, which completely covers the steel surface and seals it from corrosive environments, this is unachievable using any “spray on” method.
Rated Recovery Points
Rated recovery points on the X1’s rear bar are integrated into the chassis transferring load back to the towbar to ensure rear recoveries are safe and easy.
Adjustable Camber & Toe
The X1’s suspension is fully adjustable in both camber and toe allowing the suspension to absorb any large impact without damaging any components.
Optional 200mm Extended Drawbar
Extended drawbar available as an option to accommodate for full articulation around the vehicle when attached to a tray back ute where the towbar is under the tray.
DO35 Hitchmaster Coupling
The “drop-on” hitch comes into its own when trying to couple up on uneven and soft surfaces where trying to level up the tow vehicle and caravan or camper trailer can be extremely difficult. Includes integrated handbrake.
Laser Cut Bottle Opener
Confirming our Australian thought process in design, beer bottles go hand in hand with camping in the Australian bush!
33” Mud Terrain Tyres
Federal 285/75 R16 Mud Terrain tyres provide the ultimate grip in any offroad situation.
STEEL 16 x 8 Wheels
Featuring a tough steel construction in a Black powdercoat finish.
