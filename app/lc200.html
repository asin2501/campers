ICON Remote Resevoir Adjustable SuSPENSION

Using ICON remote resevoir coilover suspension, the Patriot Supertourers will deal with any road or track you want to throw at it. Matched with springs to suit your vehicle weight requirements up to 4inches of lift is safely available providing the lift you require to conquer any extreme terrain. Attack the outback or just cruise the urban streets with this all-new military styled wheel from ROH. ROH Octagon comes in stealthy full matt black or matt black / bright machined for that tough off-road look. Octagon surpasses Australian Standard AS1638 for guaranteed off-road performance and up to 1,250kg load rating.

05.

LEAD THE PACK

AND STAY ON TRACK

Stick to your route or make your own with the Patriot Campers LC200’s proven navigation & communication set up.

The top of the line GME TX3550S combined with the Hema HN7 are the perfect solution when roaming into the unknown and remote regions of Australia.

GME TX 3550S

Bringing together all of GME's unique technology, including ScanSuite™; the latest in digital scanning technology; the TX3550S is the flagship of the S series family.

The radio comes with 95 user programmable receive frequencies to listen in on local emergency services to keep safe and stay in touch. Other features include SelCall, CTCSS, DCS and Voice Inversion Scrambler for those users looking for greater privacy and less interruption.

The Hema Navigator HN7

The Hema Navigator HN7 packages Australia’s most trusted touring and off-road navigation inside a 7-inch finger-touch screen.

Explore Australia with street and 4WD navigation, over 6000 camping and touring points of interest (POI) and brand new campsite photos that maximise the HN7’s large high-resolution display.
